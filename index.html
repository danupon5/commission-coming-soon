Monthly target

Commission is paid only once cumulative achievement reaches at least 50% of the cumulative monthly target. Targets below are cumulative: Month N target = (Quota ÷ 12) × N.

Month M1 target (USD) M1 min to get paid (50%, USD) M1 target (THB) M1 min to get paid (50%, THB) M2 target (USD) M2 min to get paid (50%, USD) M2 target (THB) M2 min to get paid (50%, THB)

Formula reference

  1. Commission THB (100% achievement) = Salary M1 ÷ Salary% × Commission% × 12
  2. Achieve paid (THB) per tier = Commission THB (100%) × (tier's ratio ÷ total of Ratio M1+M2)
  3. Commission (THB) per tier = (Project GP/Rev of tier (USD) ÷ Quota of tier (USD)) × Achieve paid (THB) of that tier
  4. Commission (USD) per tier = Commission (THB) ÷ Exchange rate
  5. Achievement % per tier = Project GP/Rev (USD) ÷ Quota (USD) × 100
  6. Total commission = sum of M1 + M2 commission (THB and USD)
  7. Achievement scenarios (50/75/100/125/150%) = Quota × scenario% gives the GP/Rev target; the same % × Achieve paid (THB) gives the commission at that achievement level
  8. Monthly target (Month N) = (Quota M1 or M2 ÷ 12) × N (cumulative); THB target = USD target × Exchange rate; min to get paid (50%) = target × 0.5 in USD and THB; commission is only paid once cumulative GP/Rev reaches at least 50% of that month's target
